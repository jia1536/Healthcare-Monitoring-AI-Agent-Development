# Diabetes Overview

Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body cannot properly regulate blood sugar (glucose) levels.

## Types
- **Type 1**: The body produces little or no insulin. Usually diagnosed in children and young adults.
- **Type 2**: The body becomes resistant to insulin or doesn't produce enough. Most common form, linked to lifestyle and genetics.
- **Gestational**: Develops during pregnancy and usually resolves after delivery, but raises future Type 2 risk.

## Common Symptoms
- Frequent urination
- Excessive thirst
- Unexplained weight loss
- Fatigue
- Blurred vision
- Slow-healing wounds

## Management
- Regular blood glucose monitoring
- Balanced diet with controlled carbohydrate intake
- Physical activity
- Medication or insulin therapy as prescribed by a doctor
- Regular checkups for eyes, kidneys, and feet
