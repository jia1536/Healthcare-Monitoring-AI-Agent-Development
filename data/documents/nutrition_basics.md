# Nutrition & Wellness Basics

## Balanced Diet Fundamentals
- Include a mix of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals
- Prioritize whole grains, vegetables, fruits, legumes, and lean protein
- Limit added sugars, deep-fried foods, and highly processed snacks
- Stay hydrated — water intake needs vary by activity level and climate

## Indian Dietary Context
- Traditional Indian diets often provide good fiber and protein through dals, whole grains (roti, brown rice), and vegetables
- Be mindful of oil quantity and refined carbohydrates (white rice, maida-based snacks) in daily meals
- Consider regional variations — dietary patterns differ significantly across states

## General Activity Guidance
- Aim for regular moderate activity most days of the week (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
- Combine cardio with light strength training for overall fitness
- Prioritize consistent sleep (7-9 hours for most adults) as part of overall wellness
